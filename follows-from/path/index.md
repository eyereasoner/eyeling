# The Path

The path is the side of unfolding.

It stands for origin and evolution, for what begins, persists, branches, transforms, and continues. It is not a route laid across a finished map. It is the world in its own becoming.

## What belongs here

- origin
- evolution
- persistence
- transformation
- inheritance
- constraints
- dynamics
- forward consequence

## Relation to Follows From

The path gives Follows From its generative side: every artifact does not only reconstruct what is given, it also derives consequences and follows how structures unfold.
