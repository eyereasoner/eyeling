# The Method

The method is how a question becomes a trustworthy artifact.

Follows From adopts a simple discipline:

- **Answer** — what is being claimed
- **Reason** — why the claim follows
- **Check** — how the claim is independently tested

## Core sequence

**Trace → Question → Model → Answer → Reason → Check**

Or more compactly:

**Ask backward. Derive forward. Check openly.**

## Design principles

1. Start from a real question.
2. Keep assumptions visible.
3. Prefer small structures over opaque complexity.
4. Make the line from data to conclusion explicit.
5. Build the check into the artifact itself.
6. Treat clarity as part of rigor.

## Relation to Follows From

The method is where philosophy becomes practice. It is the operational bridge between the library’s questions and the path’s consequences.
