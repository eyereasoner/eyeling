=== Answer ===
Euler's identity holds exactly in this exact-arithmetic model: exp(i*pi) + 1 = 0.

=== Reason Why ===
exp(i*pi) is represented as (-1, 0) and adding (1, 0) gives the exact zero complex number.
exp(i*pi)   : (-1, 0)
exp(i*pi)+1 : (0, 0)
|exp(i*pi)|^2: 1

=== Check ===
identity exact: yes
unit circle   : yes
