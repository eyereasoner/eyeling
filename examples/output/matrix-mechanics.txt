In this toy matrix-mechanics model, the Hamiltonian has two discrete energy levels and does not commute with a second observable.

=== Reason Why ===
H = [[1,0],[0,2]]
X = [[0,1],[1,0]]
HX = [[0,1],[2,0]]
XH = [[0,2],[1,0]]
[H,X] = [[0,-1],[1,0]]

trace/determinant match energy levels: yes
